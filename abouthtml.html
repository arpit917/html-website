ABOUT HTML



  • HTML stands for Hyper Text Markup Language .
  • It describes the structure of a Web page .
  • It consists of a series of elements .
  • Its elements tell the browser how to display the content .
  • Its elements are represented by tags .
  • Its tags label pieces of content such as "heading", "paragraph", "table",etc. Browsers do not display the HTML tags, but use them to render the content of the page .
